# Character-Embedded Game List

## Mascot Concept

Working name: **Mimi**
Placeholder only. Can be changed later.

I have a website idea for unserious internet games for boredom, funn, delusion, and tiny bad decisions with HTML/CSS/JS. (kind of a troll)

And I have a mascot (playable character) for the games, it's Mimi.

Mimi is a cute, slightly chubby, silly, energetic character. She is clumsy, curious, dramatic in a harmless way, and knows how to laugh at herself. She is not “perfect,” not too cool, not too polished, and not cringe. She represents the playful childish part of every human being: the part that overthinks texts, makes tiny bad decisions, gets bored, wants to have fun, and survives life through humor.

Important personality rules:

* She is funny because she is sincere, not stupid.
* She is clumsy, but not helpless.
* She makes mistakes, but laughs and keeps going.
* She is energetic, but not annoying.
* She is emotionally dramatic, but self-aware.
* She should not feel like a stereotype of girls.
* She should feel like a mascot for everyone’s inner chaos.

The site can use her in three ways:

1. **Playable character** in games.
2. **Guide/narrator** in quizzes and generators.
3. **Result character** in outcome screens, stickers, reactions, and animations.

# Final Brand Direction

The character makes the project better if she becomes the emotional center of the site.

Without Mimi:
The site is a list of funny tools.

With Mimi:
The site is a funny little world where one chaotic mascot survives texting, red flags, boredom, delusion, plans, apologies, and tiny disasters.

That is more memorable, more brandable, and better for future visual design.
